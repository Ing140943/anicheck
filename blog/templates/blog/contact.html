About Us


Web application for people who are interested and love to watch Japanese animation. The main purpose of our application is to support the copyright of Japanese animation. We hope that our web application will give encouragement to every animation maker.



Vision


For the people who want to find some copyright sources of the anime that they are interested in, and want to create their favorite anime list. They will know some detail about that anime such as the number of episodes, some main characters, etc. They can also see some reviews of that anime by the other users that will help them get more information about that anime.
