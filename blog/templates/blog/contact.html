About Us

People who are interested in and enjoy watching Japanese anime can use this web application.
The primary goal of our application is to protect Japanese anime copyright.
We believe that our web application will provide inspiration to all animators.



Vision


For people who wish to make a favorite anime list and want to find some copyright sources for the anime they're interested in.
They will be aware of some details of the anime, such as the amount of episodes, the main characters, and so on.
They can also read reviews of the anime written by other people, which can help them learn more about it.



Background


We now have a plethora of online options for watching Japanese animation.
However, many consumers are unaware of which providers have anime that they want to watch.
Also, stay away from piracy apps and websites that cater to anime creators.
We can search from any search engine using this project, although they frequently return results that are connected
but not on the user's direct primary point, and they occasionally return results from piracy applications or websites.
The goal of this web application is to get consumers to view anime using legal and copyright apps.
